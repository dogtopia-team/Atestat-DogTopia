        DogTopia story started from a strong passion for dogs, but over time it became a powerful project we never thought we will attach so much to. The first DogTopia store appeared in Iasi, Romania, in 2014. DogTopia proved to be a real succes and it got a lot of popularity among the dog lovers. With 5 years experience, DogTopia has learned that every single dog is unic and needs special treatment.

        The vivid color decoration, the unique arrangement, the well prepared staff and the pleasent atmosphere are just a few of the reasons our clients come back to our stores. To better help you, DogTopia is offering you dozens of discounts and special promotions for a wide varity of products!

        Thus, we're very proud of the thousands of dogs we've made happy, the hundreds of smiles we put to your faces and the lots of amazing stories of our clients that proved to us every time that the human-dog bond is indestructible.
